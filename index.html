How to Use:

First click on the screen (this is needed to start the audio context to allow your browser to play the sound). From there, it's as easy as pressing any of the keys on your computer keyboard that correspond to the characters listed on each synthesizer example key.

You can change the playable note range with the first dropdown selector or change the waveform with the second dropdown selector
